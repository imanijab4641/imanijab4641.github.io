FAQ

Common questions

How do I send you my raw footage?
Completely up to you — Google Drive, WeTransfer, Dropbox, Frame.io, or any other platform you prefer. I'll work with whatever is most convenient for your workflow.
How long does a project take?
It depends on the complexity and length of the video. A short-form Reel might be done in 1–2 days, while a longer YouTube video or corporate film may take 3–7 days. We'll agree on a timeline before I start work.
How is pricing decided?
I don't use a fixed price list because every project is different. After you tell me about your video — its length, complexity, and what you need — I'll give you a fair, transparent quote with no hidden costs.
What format will you deliver in?
Whatever you need — MP4 (H.264/H.265), ProRes, or any other format. I'll also export at the correct resolution and aspect ratio for your target platform (YouTube, Instagram, etc.).
Do you work with clients outside India?
Absolutely. I work with clients worldwide. Everything happens online — briefing, file transfer, feedback, and delivery — so location is never a barrier.
