Hi, I'm Subin — a freelance video editor based in Trivandrum, Kerala, working with content creators and brands across the world. I believe that great editing is invisible: it should make the viewer feel something, not notice the technique behind it.

My approach is built on clear communication and affordable, transparent rates. I take the time to understand your vision before touching the timeline, and I keep you informed throughout the process — no surprises, no guesswork.

I edit entirely in DaVinci Resolve Studio 21 on a MacBook Pro M4 Pro, giving me a professional, fast, and colour-accurate workflow from the first import to the final export.

Process

How I work

1

Brief & Discovery

We start with a conversation. I learn about your project, your audience, your goals, and any references you love. The more context I have, the better the edit.

2

Footage Review & Plan

I review all the raw footage and assets you share, then map out an editing approach tailored to the complexity and format of your video.

3

Edit & Refine

I build the cut — pacing, colour, audio, graphics — and send you a draft for review. Your feedback shapes every revision.

4

Final Delivery

Once approved, I export in the format and resolution you need and deliver via your preferred platform — ready to publish.
